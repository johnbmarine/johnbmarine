Blogging

My primary trade is blogging. I used to blog a lot on Myspace when Myspace was still popular in its day. I later decided to start a blog on the Blogger/Blogspot platform. If you were to read my Blogger profile, it will say that I have been blogging since May 2007. However, I did not post my very first blog post on my main blog until January 2009. I have blogged ever since and even established five other blogs (one of which discontinued) on the Blogger/Blogspot platform. I have enjoyed having an international audience view my blogging work over the years. Since 2009 or so, I have gotten blog hits from many countries- at least 180 different countries. As someone who loves stars and space, I adopted a theme of stars and space to my blog names.

If you would like to visit any or all of my blogs, I invite you to click on the "visit" links below each blog's description.
